# Funding and Team

Browser-use was founded by Magnus Müller and Gregor Žunič [[2]](#ref-2) [[4]](#ref-4), who met while studying at ETH Zurich [[3]](#ref-3) [[4]](#ref-4). The company is part of the Y Combinator W25 batch [[6]](#ref-6) and is based in San Francisco [[5]](#ref-5).

## Funding
In March 2025, Browser-use raised **$17 million** in funding to accelerate the development of its web agent technology [[2]](#ref-2) [[3]](#ref-3) [[6]](#ref-6).

## Founders
* **Magnus Müller**: Co-founder who previously worked on web-scraping tools for several years before starting the project [[2]](#ref-2).
* **Gregor Žunič**: Co-founder who met Müller during their graduate studies at ETH Zurich [[4]](#ref-4).

## Background
Both founders were data science graduate students at **ETH Zurich** [[3]](#ref-3). The project was initially developed through the **ETH Zurich Student Project House** accelerator [[2]](#ref-2). Browser-use is also part of the **Y Combinator Winter 2025 (W25)** cohort [[6]](#ref-6).

## Team and Hiring
As of early 2025, the team consists of approximately **7 employees** based in San Francisco [[5]](#ref-5). The company is actively seeking **exceptional engineers** to expand its team, specifically hiring for **3 engineering roles** to further develop its AI automation capabilities [[1]](#ref-1) [[5]](#ref-5).
